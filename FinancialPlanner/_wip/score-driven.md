### Factor shares time varing present value model

y - output  
f - factor share dividend growth minus output growth
e - equity risk premium
r* - natural interest rate


$$

\overline{dp}_t = \={y}_t + \={f}_t - log(exp(r* + \={e}_t) - exp(\={y}_t + \={f}_t))  \\
\Delta{d} = y_t + f_t \\
y_t = \={y}_t + \~{y}_t \\
f_t = \={f}_t + \~{f}_t \\


$$
